About Us

Atlanta Ratpack started as a group of 1st generation American kids who loved sports. We formed lifelong bonds through baseball, basketball, and football. Starting in high school we competed in tournaments in the Muslim and Asian communities. We quickly became one of the top teams in the Southeast. We have consistently won highly competitive tournaments for over a decade in both Basketball & Flag Football.

As 1st generation Americans, our parents valued education over everything. We came to the realization that we did not have the support from our parents to pursue our passion of sports.

Although we have achieved a great amount of success, for many of us there remained the question of “What If”.

  • What if my parents supported my sports ambitions?
  • What if we received formal training for these sports?
  • What if we were given opportunities to network with likeminded youth?


COACHING STAFF

The Atlanta Ratpack coaching staff consists of current AAU coaches and members who have experience in high school, college, and professional level sports credentials.

We welcome students of all backgrounds and skill levels. We strive to create a friendly and safe environment centered on self-improvement.

MISSION:

To inspire health and wellness and strengthen our communities.

VISION:

To transform communities by inspiring youth through sports and fitness. We will promote an active/healthy lifestyle by offering training and camps that allow youth to network, learn, and grow.

VALUES:

  • Increase health and wellness
  • Promote networking among youth
  • Establish strong leadership skills
  • Develop communication skills, team work, and discipline
  • Increase exposure and guidance with targeted and specific training
